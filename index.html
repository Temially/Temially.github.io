smoothie it up!

banana, pineapple & coconut smoothie

This combo of Iron, Fiber and Vitamin C makes for a refreshing tropical taste!

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups of pineapples, cut into chunks
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup Coconut water
  • 1 cup ice(crushed)

    • Add all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Enjoy!

Servings: 2 smoothies

**Recipe can be doubled as needed**
